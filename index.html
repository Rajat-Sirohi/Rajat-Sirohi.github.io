Memories

  • He would lick us all over - Deepti
  • He would come and butt his head to seek attention
  • The first day, he came and sat next to us and won us over in less than one hour - Rajat
  • I was so mad Ruchi was getting him (due to his age) but he became the love of my life - Niren
  • He would come up to each of us individually and spread his love
  • When he sat with Deepti and then I came, he would dump her and run to me - Niren
  • In the first week, we thought we lost him. However, after a lot of searching, we foud him cuddled up in a box in the nook - Rajat
  • He would always find new places to lay down, then get bored and find new places
  • He got stuck under the stairs behind the curtain in the basement and we could not find him
  • He would sit on bags, that we would put on the surfboard and pull like a chariot - Rajat
  • He would jump behind the TV and get stuck, forcing us to retrieve him
  • We would set up the tunnel for Norman and he would sit down on top of it
  • He would manipulate me to come to pet him while eating by swatting Normandy - Niren
  • He recognized us in the hospital and wanted to get out of his cage to come home with us
  • He would want to enter Ruchi's room in the morning and we would crack open the door so he could jump in bed with her
  • He would sit on my laptop as I worked, forcing me to pet him instead - Rajat
  • We would throw toys up the stairs and he would run up and knock it back down
  • He would play by himself in the morning sometimes run up to lower steps, roll over and make happy noises and then suddenly run super fast into the living room or his room as if he was chasing something
  • He did not like being in my lap... except that last day in the hospital when he let me hug him and hold him tight next to my chest. What a lovebug... always wanted to make you happy... - Niren
  • He would like to kill things with his right back leg as if he was going in for the attack
  • Sometimes he tried to bite Normy's ear but only when we were around. If were not around he always left Normy alone
  • He would sit on my lap in the basement chairs - Rajat
  • When I came home from office, he would come and greet me from wherever he was - Niren
  • He would sit on Rajat's chair in his room
  • He loved drinking water out of both taps in the upstairs bathroom
  • He would always clean in the sink before sitting down
  • He would sit and stare at you when we wanted food
  • He had two different meows: hungry and playful
  • He never ever got angry. He was very forgiving
  • He loved tummy rubs!
  • He would push away with his leg if he did not want a rub
  • The jingle of his collar as he walked
  • He would lie down in the basement and sleep in bed with me - Rajat
  • Whenever we were fixing anything, he would come and try to help, as if he was an expert insepecting our work
  • When we took him outside, he would sit next to the screen door and watch birds and animals
  • He would cry in the morning inside his room waiting for the door to open
  • As soon as the door opened, I would pet him and he would walk to the small box and scratch his front leg on the box - Niren
  • He loved getting rubbed by the beard and loved getting hugged in the hospital
  • He sat right in front of closed doors so we had to put up signs to prevent hitting him while opening them
  • He sat at the screen door and watched Deepti and Rajat play badminton, with his head closely following the birdie
  • He loved his sample carpet for his stairs
  • I loved kissing and hugging him as well as stroking his tail when he ate
  • He would come up and sit in bed with Deepti
  • When we slept with him in his room he was uncomfortable, he preferred his privacy
  • He would get up whenever anybody came in the room
  • He gave unconditional love to everybody
  • He sat and slept at the window in the sun
  • He loved exploring in the car, sitting up and looking out the windows and sitting in the front seat
  • He would come up and lie in master bed. He spent so many Saturday mornings laying in bed with Niren
  • In the summer, he would be desperate to go outside even though we didn't want him to
  • He would love to do things you did not want him to, a rebellious yet loving cat
  • Tummy rubs were his favorite. He would grab your arm with his front and back leg and not let go. If you pulled out your arm, he would turn up his head and look at you with a death stare until you returned your arm to him
  • He loved playing with rubber bands. One would throw it up high and he would catch it in his mouth
  • He loved playing and running on brown paper from boxes. If one threw a rubber band on it he would kick it off but not chase it outside the region of paper. He had marked it as his territory
  • He had such a graceful walk, like a supermodel
  • He loved getting covered up with blankets in the winter
  • Sometimes he would really love going after Norman and we would have to lock him up in his room to keep them apart. We couldn't stay mad at him though :)
  • He would run after us when we brought food from the kitched to his room
  • When Rajat was at UMass Amherst and Deepti and Ruchi were going to Cornell, Pixel had escaped through the back door to under the front steps. I was frantically running around shouting for Pixel in the yard - Niren
  • He loved sitting in Norman's boxes
  • After we would close Norman in his cage, Pixel would still think he was in the box and search for him
  • Normy had double-decker boxes and Pixel would sit in the top level
  • He was most vocal in the mornings
  • When he first came home, he and Normy would rub noses and love each other to get introduced
  • When Normy got sick, Pixel would show compassion by lying down in front his cage
  • He loved drinking from Normy's bowl, in alignment with his rebellious nature
  • He would run like a tiger with rubber bands and foil balls
  • He react furiously with Dr. Daria Smith by making loud noises
  • When we got him all kinds of expensive toys/towers, he didn't care and preferred garbage plastic bags and cardboard boxes
  • When we got him catnip he couldn't care less
  • He was a simple cat for sure...
